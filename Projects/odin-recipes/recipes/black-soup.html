Black Soup


Craftable using loot and farm from the Swamp Biome.
Item description: A perfect balance of sweetness and acidity.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bloodbag (from Leeches)
  • 1 Honey
  • 1 Turnip (after obtaining and farming Turnip Seeds)

Effects:

  1. Max Health: 50
  2. Max Stamina: 17
  3. Duration: 1200s
  4. Healing: 3hp/tick
